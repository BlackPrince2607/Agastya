Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 6, 2026

Agastya ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Agastya mobile application and associated services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Agastya is designed for entertainment and personal reflection only. We do not provide medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile data you provide: display name, gender (optional), and focus areas you choose during onboarding.
  • Palm image: a photo taken during the palm-scan step. This image is sent to our servers solely to generate your reading and may be stored in a private cloud bucket tied to your session. It is never shared with third parties or used for training AI models.
  • Session and device identifiers: a randomly generated session ID and device install ID used to restore your reading across app launches.
  • Email address (optional): only if you choose to sign in for cross-device sync. We do not send marketing email without your explicit opt-in.
  • Analytics events: pseudonymous event data (e.g. "paywall viewed", "reading generated") sent to PostHog or Mixpanel. No real name, email, or payment data is included in analytics events.
  • Crash reports: error and crash data sent to Sentry, including device type and app version. No personal content is included.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and display your personalised palm reading, daily tasks, predictions, and AI Guide responses.
  • To save your reading and restore it across devices when you sign in.
  • To process subscription payments via RevenueCat and Apple/Google billing. We do not store payment card data.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app reliability via Sentry.
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns via anonymous analytics.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — database and storage provider for session data and palm images.
  • Groq — AI inference provider for palm analysis and guide responses. Data sent to Groq is governed by their privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Governed by their privacy policy.
  • Apple / Google — in-app purchase processing.
  • Sentry — crash reporting. No PII in payloads.
  • PostHog / Mixpanel — analytics. Pseudonymous identifiers only.

4. Data Retention

Session data is retained for up to 12 months of inactivity. Palm images are retained for up to 90 days. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@agastya.app.

6. Children

Agastya is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Palm images are stored in a private bucket with service-role access only. We regularly review our security practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by email.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests: privacy@agastya.app
