## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

Agastya reads your palm and reveals the patterns woven into your life — your love language, career potential, growth edges, and what's ahead. Powered by AI, grounded in palmistry tradition, and built to guide you daily.

**Your palm, decoded.**
Scan your palm in seconds. Agastya analyses your life, heart, and head lines to build a personalised report across love, career, money, and personal growth. No two readings are alike — yours is tied to your unique hand and the intentions you set.

**Your full reading, unlocked.**
- Complete palm report across four life domains
- Compatibility insights — see how your energy connects with someone else
- Daily predictions: weekly, monthly, and annual forecasts
- Unlimited conversations with your AI Guide, Agastya

**Your AI Guide.**
Agastya is more than a reading — it's a conversation. Ask about your relationships, career crossroads, or what a palm line really means for you. Your Guide responds with context from your unique reading, not generic advice.

**Daily rituals.**
Small, palm-aligned actions delivered each morning. Not a to-do list — a living practice shaped by who you actually are.

**Built with intention.**
- Private: your palm image is stored securely and never used to train AI
- Offline-capable: your reading works without a connection
- Cross-device: sign in to restore your reading on any device
- For entertainment and reflection — not medical or professional advice
